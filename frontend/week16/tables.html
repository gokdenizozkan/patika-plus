Animal Kingdom

Welcome to the fascinating world of animals! From mammals to reptiles and birds, the animal kingdom is diverse and incredible.

Mammals

Mammals are a diverse group of animals that include humans, dogs, and whales. They are characterized by the presence of mammary glands and hair or fur on their bodies.

Reptiles

Reptiles, such as snakes, lizards, and turtles, are cold-blooded vertebrates. They are known for their scales and often lay eggs. Some reptiles, like crocodiles, live in aquatic environments.

Birds

Birds are feathered, warm-blooded animals that include species like eagles, penguins, and sparrows. They have wings and beaks and are adapted for various forms of flight.
