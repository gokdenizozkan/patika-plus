Menemen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent.
  3. Add green pepper and continue to sauté until it softens.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until they release their juices.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  6. Make wells in the mixture and crack the eggs into each well.
  7. Cover the pan and cook until the eggs are done to your liking.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Menemen!

Note: Feel free to customize the recipe to your taste preferences.
